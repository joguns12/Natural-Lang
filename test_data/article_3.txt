Kathy drives to an abandoned mining camp on the outskirts of the city and dumps Steve's body down a mine shaft. Donner returns that night and continues to question Kathy about Wendell's disappearance, causing her to become evasive. That evening, Kathy books a flight to Denver. In the airport, she overhears a page on the intercom for Wendell, and glimpses a man resembling him at a bar, causing her to flee.
